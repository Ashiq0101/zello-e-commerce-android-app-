# AI Assistance Declaration

This project was developed with the assistance of several Artificial Intelligence tools. While the core logic, design decisions, and overall architecture were driven by human effort, AI models were utilized as collaborative assistants throughout the development lifecycle.

Specifically, tools such as **Claude AI**, **ChatGPT**, and **Antigravity** provided valuable support in the following areas:

*   **Ideation and Problem Solving:** Brainstorming approaches to complex features and architectural patterns.
*   **Debugging and Code Refinement:** Assisting in identifying bugs, troubleshooting errors, and optimizing code snippets.
*   **Concept Clarification:** Exploring best practices and providing syntax examples for various frameworks and libraries.

These AI tools served as pair programmers, helping to accelerate development and overcome technical roadblocks. The final implementation, testing, and integration are the result of dedicated human direction and oversight.
